# Solutions-Nielsen-Chuang
These are handwritten study notes and personal solutions to exercises from 
*Nielsen & Chuang - Quantum Computation ans Quantum Information.

The notes were written using GoodNotes on an iPad and uploaded as PDFs.

The first chapters currently available (CH. 5 and CH. 8) are written in Spanish,
as they correspond to my original personal study notes.

Future material may appear either in Spanish or English.

Feedback and corrections are always welcome.

These notes were written while studying the book independentlyu as part of my
training in quantum computing.
